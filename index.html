About us

CodeSprint is a high-energy 24-hour hackathon designed to challenge and inspire developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts. Participants form teams to tackle innovative projects, solve real-world problems, and create cutting-edge technology. This event features mentorship from industry experts, networking opportunities, and exciting prizes. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a newcomer, CodeSprint is the ultimate test of your coding skills and creativity
